# Chapter: Epilogue

As developments in classification aim for increased accuracy, efficiency, and scalability, there's a growing emphasis on AI ethics, transparency, and fairness in algorithmic decision-making.

While we come across many books on regression regression, we do not find as many books dedicated to classificaion only. 

This book is primarily targeted at data scientists early in their careers.
